While many liberties needed to be taken in the creation of Network Graphs (such as cleaning those users who did not list information and only choosing search terms with not too many but not too few remaining users) the insights identified below still form a starting point for further analysis and it is not certain that the visualised data is inherently biased though the low proportion of included data precludes indicating 'results' were found.

Topic Networks by Age Group

Only the 25 most popular topics are included.





Insights

More senior stack overflow members assist younger members who often originate questions. Mid-level members (i.e. 35 to 50) often interact with each other, likely discussing more advanced topics which junior members would not ask and potentially senior members do not immediately know the answer to because it is a specialised question (these are initial insights and would need to be verified
